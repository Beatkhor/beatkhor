About Us

Since 2017, Beatkhor aims to promote creativity and accelerate the development of the music culture. All the beats are made by the beatkhor community.

Our Mission

Beatkhor is dedicated to promoting creativity and collaboration in the music industry. Our mission is to provide a free platform for emerging producers to share their beats, enabling vocalists worldwide to explore unique sounds for their artistic expression. We aim to democratize music creation, bridging the gap between talent and opportunity. Through BeatKhor, we envision a vibrant community where every beat finds its voice, and every voice finds its beat.

Our Story

BeatKhor's story began in 2017, when I was just a young boy with a passion for music production. With limited resources and no industry connections, I faced an uphill battle. Despite not being a professional, I poured my heart into my music, using it as a means of personal expression. I yearned to share my beats with the world, but the high costs of publishing platforms were insurmountable.

Navigating the music industry was challenging. It often seemed like those with industry connections were always a step ahead, and for an introverted individual like myself who simply desired to express my creativity through music, making such connections was daunting.

Faced with these hurdles, an idea began to form: a platform that could empower producers like me, offering them the ability to publish and promote their music without the financial burden. A platform that could level the playing field, ensuring that talent, not money or connections, took center stage.

This idea sparked the inception of BeatKhor. A free platform designed to encourage creative expression, democratize the music industry, and give a voice to talented producers who may not have the means to share their work. Here at BeatKhor, we believe in the power of music and its ability to connect people, regardless of their background or circumstances. Welcome to our journey, where every beat has its stage and every voice its song."
